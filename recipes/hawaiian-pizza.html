Joey's Hawaiian Pizza

The Hawaiian pizza with a little kick that'll knock your socks off!

We're never shy here at Joey's Pizza Pies when it comes to talking our own book. But believe me when I tell you this Hawaiian pizza receipt will blow your mind. The saltiness of the ham. The sweetness of the pineapple. The jalapenos' heat. It's a flavor explosion in your mouth that'll leave you coming back for more.

As always, check out all the ingredients you need and steps to chef up this delicious pizza pie below.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of store-bought pizza-dough.
  • 1 jar of store-bought pizza sauce.
  • 1 package of store-bought mozzerella cheese.
  • 1 bottle of extra-virgin olive oil.
  • 1 package of store-bought Canadian bacon.
  • 1 store-bought pineapple.
  • 3-4 jalapeno peppers

Steps

  1. Pre-heat your oven as high as it goes (ideally 500 degrees F). When cooking pizza, less time at higher temp will help achieve a crisper crust, avoid sogginess, and provide more even cooking.
  2. Place your pizza stone inside the oven, letting it pre-heat alongside the oven.
  3. Put your pizza dough in a bowl, and place it in a warm area while your oven pre-heats. This relaxes the gluten structure of the dough, making it easier to roll out.
  4. Once your oven's pre-heated, place some flour on your countertop and roll out the dough into the always classic, circle.
  5. Spread sauce onto your dough, making sure not to use too much.
  6. Place a generous amount of mozzerella cheese on the pizza, making sure there is even distribution.
  7. Slice several slices of Canadian bacon into 1 inch cubes. Apply generously to the top of the pizza.
  8. Slice the pineapple into one inch cubes. Place a generous amount to the top of the pizza.
  9. Enjoy some extra pineapple on the side. It's pineapple. It's delicious.
  10. Chop the jalapenos into fairly thin slices. Place them on the pizza.
  11. Remove the pizza stone from the oven and put the pizza on it. Immediately place back in the oven.
  12. Cook the pizza for whatever seems like a good amount of time.
  13. Remove from oven, and slice into the triangles we all know and love.
  14. Enjoy!
